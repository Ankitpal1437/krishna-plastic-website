About Krishna Plastic

A Unique Startup With An Innovative Vision

Krishna Plastic is a unique startup from Rajkot with a philosophy of innovative ideas to make a big fortune. We believe that a good idea makes good business — and following this ideology, we have developed a wide range of high-quality plastic products that enhance home-making easier and more enjoyable.
